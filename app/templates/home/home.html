Caihong

AI-hieroglyph generator

Caihong consist of neural network and png2svg tracer. The neural network was trained on 5K Korean hieroglyphs and produce 64x64 png image. Then png2svg tracer converts the image into svg, so you can scale the hieroglyph the way you like.
